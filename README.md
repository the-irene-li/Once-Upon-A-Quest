# Once Upon a Quest
Embark on a magical learning adventure, and let your wonder guide the way.

Targeted at children ages 5 to 11, Once Upon a Quest is an app that posing learning as an adventure, featuring built-in break times, real-life outside exploration, an interactive buddy avatar that answers questions, and more! With Once Upon a Quest, children embark on quests that feed their curiosity and learn new concepts. We aim to tackle and remove the fear and anxiety of making mistakes, instead encouraging children to be guided by their curiosity and be motivated to learn.
